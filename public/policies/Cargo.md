# Cargo Policy

As a socially and environmentally responsible company, the Company is committed to taking a more sustainable development path for present and future generations while engaged in the carriage and transportation of cargo for our customers.

The promotion of safety and environmental objectives while engaged in cargo carriage and transportation is of particular focus. The Company will at all times comply with all regulations laid down by relevant insurance bodies, international maritime organizations, and conventions such as:

- MARPOL (International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution from Ships)
- SOLAS (International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea)
- ILO
- OCIMF
- SIGTTO
- ICS
- INTERTANKO
- BIMCO

as well as all other relevant international and national regulatory requirements to ensure safe sea transportation of cargo.

The Company emphasizes its commitment to preventing:

- Sanctions evasion  
- Smuggling  
- Criminal activity  
- Facilitation of terrorist activities  
- Proliferation of weapons of mass destruction (WMD)  

The Company will carry out due diligence, at a minimum, on:

- Companies owned by or registered to individuals with no previous international trading background.
- Shipments in which the declared counterparty is newly registered or has no apparent background in or knowledge of relevant lines of business.
- Shipments in which relevant counterparty and payment details are illogical or anomalous.

The Company’s vessels will not engage in the transportation of illegal or prohibited products or the carriage of waste materials.

Furthermore, the Company requires all vessels engaged in sea transportation of cargo to be classed by a member of the International Association of Classification Societies (IACS) to ensure vessels are maintained at all times in accordance with international best practices and standards. Compliance will be ensured through regular ship inspections.

We recognize that any policy is only as effective as it is practicable and enforceable. The Company will engage with customers and statutory bodies to ensure that this policy is understood and supported.

This policy applies to all employees and contractors employed or working on board vessels, in any capacity, and at the Company’s workplaces ashore.
