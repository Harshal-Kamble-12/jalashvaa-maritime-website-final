# Security Policy

The Company will:

- Provide safe and secure work environment on vessels
- Implement measures needed to prevent unlawful actions on board
- Cooperate with international bodies and states in their endeavour to deter maritime terrorism
- Extend necessary support to the following in carrying out their responsibilities effectively:
  - Company Security Officer (CSO)
  - Master
  - Ship Security Officer (SSO)
- Assist in enhancing security awareness among the crew

Employees who are given a set of office keys for which they are personally responsible should ensure the keys at no time are in the hands of an unauthorized person.

In case of an armed hold-up or attempted robbery, employees should not put their own life or that of their colleagues at risk at any time i.e. Safety of employee takes precedence over the security. Likewise, in the event of a fire or calamity, the safety of Human life is paramount.

Detailed security procedures to comply with this policy can be found in the Security Manual of the Company’s Safety Management System and ship specific Ship Security Plans.
